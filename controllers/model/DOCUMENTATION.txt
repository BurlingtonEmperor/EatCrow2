This is the modelclave's controller. 
It is quite rudimentary and should only be used for testing purposes.

Algorithm:
If the measured temperature is lower than the desired temperature: turn heater ON
If the measured temperature is higher than the desired temperature: turn heater OFF

If the measured pressure is lower than the desired pressure: turn inlet solenoid ON and outlet solenoid OFF
If the measured pressure is higher than the desired pressure: turn outlet solenoid ON and inlet solenoid OFF
